    Winter

    Winter is the fourth season and it is followed by Spring and preceded by Autumn.

    Winter is unique amongst the seasons in that no outdoors Crops apart from Winter Seeds and Fiber Seeds will grow, and all Grass, Weeds, and most tilled soil will all be removed at the start of the season. However, the reduced intensity of farming makes it an excellent season for fishing too, Winter is tied with Summer for most birthdays in a season.

    Additionally, due to the lack of grass and weeds, Fiber is more difficulty to obtain outdoors, but you can still get fiber by planting Fiber Seeds.

    Winter season is quite different from the other 3 season since there are no plantable crops in Winter with the exception of Winter Seeds and Fiber Seeds.

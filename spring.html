    Spring

    Spring is the season when players first begin the game. It's followed by Summer and preceded by Winter. In Spring, the grass is green. Trees regain their leaves and bloom, and pink petals blow through the air on some days. There is also a variety of crops, fish which only appear during this season.

    Below are the crops that only available in Spring season.

    Potato

    The Potato is a vegetable crop that grows from Potato Seeds after 6 days.
